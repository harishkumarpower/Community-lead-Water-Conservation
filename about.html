About the Program

Our initiative focuses on empowering communities to conserve water and develop resilience to drought. We provide education, practical solutions, and funding opportunities to help local communities adopt sustainable water management practices.

About the Community-Led Water Conservation & Drought Resilience Program

The Community-Led Water Conservation & Drought Resilience Program is an initiative designed to empower communities to manage water resources efficiently and reduce the impact of droughts. It promotes sustainable water use, conservation technologies, and public awareness to ensure long-term water security.
